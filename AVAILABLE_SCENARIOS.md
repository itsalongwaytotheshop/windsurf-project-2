# Available Noise Assessment Scenarios

The Noise Assessment Wizard now includes **17 comprehensive work scenarios** extracted from the Transport for NSW spreadsheet, organized into three categories:

## Construction & Earthworks

1. **Excavation Works**
   - General excavation activities including earthmoving and trenching
   - Equipment: Excavator (105 dB), Truck (102 dB)

2. **General Road Works**
   - General road maintenance and construction activities
   - Equipment: Excavator (105 dB), Truck (102 dB), Roller (100 dB)

3. **Bridge Works**
   - Bridge construction and maintenance activities
   - Equipment: Excavator (105 dB), Breaker (115 dB), Generator (108 dB)

4. **Demolition Works**
   - Building and structure demolition activities
   - Equipment: Breaker (115 dB), Excavator (110 dB), Truck (102 dB)

5. **Concrete Works**
   - Concrete pouring and finishing activities
   - Equipment: Concrete Mixer (100 dB), Pump (95 dB), Vibrator (95 dB)

6. **Drilling Operations**
   - Drilling and core sampling activities
   - Equipment: Drill Rig (110 dB), Compressor (105 dB), Generator (108 dB)

## Road & Infrastructure

7. **Asphalt Paving**
   - Hot mix asphalt laying and compaction
   - Equipment: Paver (108 dB), Roller (100 dB), Truck (102 dB)

8. **Line Marking**
   - Road line marking and signage installation
   - Equipment: Vehicle (85 dB), Heater (90 dB), Generator (95 dB)

9. **Signage Installation**
   - Road signs and traffic signal installation
   - Equipment: Vehicle (85 dB), Auger (95 dB), Generator (90 dB)

10. **Stormwater Drainage Works**
    - Drainage installation and maintenance
    - Equipment: Excavator (105 dB), Pump (95 dB), Compactor (100 dB)

11. **Utility Installation**
    - Water, sewer, and gas line installation
    - Equipment: Excavator (105 dB), Compactor (100 dB), Generator (95 dB)

## Environmental & Maintenance

12. **Landscaping Works**
    - Landscaping and earthmoving activities
    - Equipment: Excavator (100 dB), Mower (90 dB), Blower (85 dB)

13. **Tree Removal**
    - Tree felling and vegetation clearance
    - Equipment: Chipper (100 dB), Chainsaw (110 dB), Truck (102 dB)

## Special Location Types

14. **Place of Worship**
    - Noise assessment for places of worship
   - Equipment: Paver (108 dB)

15. **Active Recreation**
    - Sports fields and active recreation areas
   - Equipment: Paver (108 dB)

16. **Passive Recreation**
    - Parks and passive recreation areas
   - Equipment: Paver (108 dB)

17. **Offices, Retail Outlets**
    - Commercial and retail areas
   - Equipment: Paver (108 dB), Roller (100 dB)

## Features

- **Sound Power Levels**: Each scenario includes typical sound power levels for all equipment
- **Equipment Details**: Shows all equipment types used in each scenario
- **Organized Categories**: Scenarios grouped by work type for easy selection
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Each scenario includes a clear description of activities

## How to Use

1. Select "Scenario" as your calculation mode in Step 2
2. In Step 7 (Work Scenario), choose from the comprehensive list
3. View equipment details and sound power levels for each scenario
4. The wizard will use the appropriate sound power levels for your calculation

## Access

The updated wizard is available at: http://localhost:3001

All scenarios are now integrated with the comprehensive noise assessment calculator, providing accurate predictions based on the specific equipment and activities involved in your project.
